About Me

I'm Charlotte R. Asis is a 32-year-old married educator and a graduate of Marikina Polytechnic College, where she earned a Bachelor of Technical Teacher Education major in Chemistry and was awarded Best Student Researcher of the Year for Batch 2016. She has nearly 10 years of teaching experience, with five years in private schools and five years in public school at Culiat High School. She teaches Science and Research to Grade 11 and 12 students and currently serves as a Grade 12 Co-chair, Science Club Adviser, and IamtheCode Teacher Facilitator.

Accomplishments

Grateful to be part of the iamtheCode PH Online Kick-off Program, an initiative that promotes innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning in technology and education, and provides an opportunity to grow professionally while contributing to a community dedicated to empowering learners and women.

Experience

Being an iamthecode teacher facilitator has been a challenging but meaningful learning journey for me. Through this role, I have learned something new in my profession and this is how to code.

Learning the basic skills of coding really challenged me at first, but it also reminded me what it feels like to be a learner, which helped me become more patient and encouraging in the classroom.

Overall, being an iamthecode teacher facilitator has strengthened my professional growth. It allowed me to learn new skills while empowering students to explore coding and believe in their abilities. This experience has shown me that teaching and learning can grow together.
